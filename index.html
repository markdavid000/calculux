Four Pillars of Calculux Ecosystem

Non-Custodial

Trade directly from your wallet app. Unlike centralized transactions exchanges like Binance or Coinbase, Calculux Exchange doesn’t hold your funds when you trade(It is NonCustodian): you have 100% ownership of your own crypto.

Affordable and Boundless Solution for Decentralization

Calculux Finance runs on Binance Smart Chain, a blockchain with much lower transaction costs than Ethereum or Bitcoin. Trading fees are lower than other top decentralized exchanges.

Unparallel Access To Decentralized Finance

People from certain region across the globe still can’t access most Centralized Exchanges for their dat to day transactions. Calculux Ecosystem is decentralized which makes it possible for anyone anywhere around the world to access and utilize our platform. Calculux is built for the people to help them achieve their financial freedom.

DAO

Calculux ecosystem is decentralized, the decisions on community development and operrations will be openly carried out by $CAX holders through voting and not influenced by any centralized entity.

Our Products

DEX

Calculux Finance is featuring advanced trading features in a totally decentralized manner on Binance Smartchain. No KYC hassle , assets remain in traders' custody. Trustless Cross-Chain Integrations: brings your crypto asset from any block chain onto Calculux Finance secured.

LAUNCHPAD

Calculuxpad is a user friendly decentralized launchpad where users can easily and seamlessly launch their on token.

NFT MARKETPLACE

Calculux NFT marketplace aim to be a fully decentralized peer-to-peer exchange that enables users to transact with one another directly in a trustless manner. Artists will list their unique digital collectibles in the form of NFTs that users can buy, sell and mint.

EDUFI

Blockchain Technology and crypto adoption are growing but the educational aspect of it is mostly being neglected and new users ends up falling victims of what they don't understand or familiar with, Calculux Edufi is a decentralized learning platform where users learn basically everything about web3.0, Blockchain Technology and crypto as a whole from basic to advanced level. At the end of any level completed, a user earn certificate as NFT, so it is more like Learn and Earn platform.
